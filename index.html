The safe room

A post-apocalyptic scene in which a lonely survivor is using this room as her shelter. Sacks are full of food rations and barrels are filled with fresh water as something as simple as water is considered luxurious in this wasteland.
One would assume that the ruined world can never see proper recovery. But time indeed heals all wounds and florets blossoming through cracks and broken windows only prove that a new world in on the horizon.
